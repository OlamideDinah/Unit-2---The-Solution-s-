Sdg 15
Life on Land


Goal 7 & Goal 15

Our world is in a climate Crisis. We are chopping down trees at an unprecedented rate, and this will end up taking a toll on our environment.

. For example, look at Brazil's amazon rainforest. Fires raged across it for months, destroying over 15% of its total land coverage.

If we hope to stop climate change, we need to plant more trees, and use less harmful technologies such as fossil fuels.
